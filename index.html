about us

made with love

We are passionate about delivering delicious and authentic pizzas, made with love and the finest ingredients. Our team of expert pizza makers takes pride in every pie that leaves our kitchen, ensuring each one is crafted to perfection. We believe that pizza should be an experience, one that brings people together and creates memories to last a lifetime. So come and join us, as we invite you to taste the difference of a pizza made with our lots of love.

30 minutes delivery

We understand that you want your pizza fast and fresh. That's why we promise to deliver your piping hot pizza straight to your door in just 30 minutes or less! Our dedicated delivery team works tirelessly to ensure that you get your meal in the quickest time possible, without sacrificing on quality. So, whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you can trust Pizza World to bring you the taste in no time. Order now and enjoy the convenience of fast and delicious pizza delivery!
